# 11 Prompt Debugging

## What it means

Prompt debugging means finding out why a prompt gives bad results and fixing the weak parts.

## When to use it

Use it when the model misunderstands the task, ignores your format, or gives low-quality answers.

## Small example

If the model keeps giving long answers, you add a rule like, "Keep each answer under 50 words."

## Example prompt

Weak prompt:
Explain marketing.

Improved prompt:
Explain marketing in under 50 words for a beginner. Use simple language and give one real-world example.

## Sample output

Marketing means helping people understand and want a product or service. For example, when a bakery posts photos of fresh cakes online to attract customers, that is marketing.

## Key takeaway

When a prompt fails, make the instruction clearer instead of starting from zero.
