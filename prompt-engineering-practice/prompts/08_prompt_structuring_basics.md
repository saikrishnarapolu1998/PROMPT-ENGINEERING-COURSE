# 08 Prompt Structuring Basics

## What it means

Prompt structuring means organizing your prompt into clear parts like role, task, context, constraints, and output format.

## When to use it

Use it when you want clear, focused, and repeatable results.

## Small example

You ask for business ideas by clearly stating who the AI is, what it should do, the user situation, the rules, and the exact output format.

## Example prompt

Role: You are a beginner-friendly business coach.
Task: Suggest 3 small business ideas.
Context: The person has a low budget and can work part-time.
Constraints: Keep the ideas realistic and simple.
Output format: Idea 1, Idea 2, Idea 3 with one reason each.

## Sample output

Idea 1: Home snack orders
Reason: It can start with low cost and basic kitchen tools.

Idea 2: Errand service
Reason: It is flexible and easy to start part-time.

Idea 3: Resume help
Reason: It has very low startup cost and is useful for job seekers.

## Key takeaway

Well-structured prompts usually give better and more predictable responses.
