# 01 Zero-Shot Prompting

## What it means

Zero-shot prompting means you ask the model to do a task without giving any examples first.

## When to use it

Use it when the task is simple and your instruction is already clear.

## Small example

If you ask, "Summarize this paragraph in 3 bullet points," without showing a sample summary, that is zero-shot prompting.

## Key takeaway

Clear instructions are often enough for simple tasks.
